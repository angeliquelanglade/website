Professional Qualifications & Insurance

As a professional member of the Federation of Holistic Therapists (FHT), I am fully qualified and insured to provide Sports Massage Therapy. Many private healthcare providers recognise FHT membership, which means you may be able to claim back the cost of your treatments with me through your health insurance. I recommend checking directly with your insurer to confirm your eligibility and any specific requirements for reimbursement.

I'm a fully qualified Level 3 and Level 4 Sports Massage Therapist, having completed my training with the highly regarded Cotswold Academy in Cirencester (2022). My qualifications allow me to assess and treat a wide range of musculoskeletal issues, from sports-related injuries to everyday aches and tension. With advanced techniques and a strong understanding of anatomy, I tailor each treatment to support recovery, enhance performance, and promote overall wellbeing.
